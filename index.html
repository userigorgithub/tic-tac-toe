SOLAR TIC TAC TOE

Welcome to a game for planet domination!

Begin to play at your own risk.

Game Rules:

  1. Upon clicking "Start" button, 1st player to start the game is randomized.
  2. Players are taking turns and clicking boxes to place their tokens.
  3. Player who can place three of their tokens horizontally, or vertically, or diagonally first, wins.
  4. When all tokens are placed and no player won, draw is declared.
  5. Game can be reset by clicking "Reset Score" or music can be turned off by clicking "Music" buttons.
  6. Good luck and have fun!
